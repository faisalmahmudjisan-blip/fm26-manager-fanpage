The Risako Way

Integrated with the Red Bull philosophy of **High-Intensity Verticality**, the system focuses on overwhelming opponents through superior fitness and wide-channel overloads. We do not wait for the game; we dictate the game.

High Intensity

Demanding 90-minute physical output. Immediate counter-press upon loss of possession.

Wide Overloads

Utilizing wing-backs and inverted wingers to create 2v1 situations in the final third.

Single Pivot

A specialized "Holding 6" to anchor the midfield, allowing the front five to attack with freedom.

System Blueprint: 4-3-3 Holding
